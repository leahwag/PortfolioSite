Sweet Tooth
April 2021

Made with candy, styrofoam, and hot glue
Class: Structure and Space

This was a project I made for a sculpture class while classes was online due to Covid . I glued together and shaped styrofoam to look like a tooth, glued candy on it, and spray painted the top. I then put some screws through a piece of wood to help it stand up. This ended up being about 1.5' tall!
