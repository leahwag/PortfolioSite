Sweet Tooth
Made with candy, styrofoam, and hot glue
April 2021

This was a project for an online art class during covid. I glued together and shaped styrofoam to look like a tooth, glued candy on it, and spray painted the top. I then put some screws through a piece of wood to help it stand up. This ended up being about 1.5' tall!
